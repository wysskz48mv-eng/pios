## 2. Patent Pending — HDCA™

### HDCA™ — Holistic Distribution & Cost Allocation Algorithm

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Full name** | Holistic Distribution & Cost Allocation Algorithm |
| **Mark** | HDCA™ |
| **Status** | Patent pending — UK Intellectual Property Office |
| **Owner** | VeritasIQ Technologies Ltd |
| **Priority date** | 2026 |
| **Jurisdictions** | United Kingdom (primary); PCT extension planned for UAE, KSA, EU |

**Core patent claims:**

1. A computer-implemented method for allocating service charge costs across multi-district master community property developments, using a configurable weighted composite of Gross Floor Area (GFA), Built-Up Area (BUA), and site area, adjusted by asset class factors (ACF) and development status factors (DSF)
2. An Equity Fairness Engine (EFE) scoring system producing a composite score (0–100) with configurable threshold (default ≥50) as a prerequisite gate for allocation runs
3. A Joint Cost Variable (JCV) mechanism for reconciling shared infrastructure costs across T1/T2/T3 governance tiers with mandatory Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) approval gates
4. An Activation Tier Parameter (AATP) system enabling phased allocation scaling across development phases (Phase 1–4) proportional to activated GFA percentage
5. COBie-compliant asset data ingestion pipeline feeding automated classification into the HDCA weighting engine
6. Multi-jurisdiction compliance layer mapping HDCA outputs to REGA/MOMRA (KSA), RERA (UAE), L&T Act (UK) regulatory submission formats

**Trade secret ref:** TS-20260323-HDCA-01
